### Example: Swampland Program

From the dispersive sum rules, we have
$$
    \mathcal{C}_{2,u}^{\text{improved}} \Big|_{\text{EFT}} = \frac{ 8\pi G }{-u} + 2 g_2 - g_3 u\,,
$$
$$
    = \langle \text{UV Averages} \rangle.
$$
Our objective is 
$$
    \text{Maximize:} \quad - \int_{0}^{1} dp f(p) \left( \frac{1}{p^2} + 2g_2 \right).
$$

Such that  
$$
    \int_{0}^{1} dp f(p) \left( \frac{1}{p^2} + 2g_2 + g_3 p^2 \right) \ge 0
$$
and 
$$
    \int_{0}^{1} dp f(p) p^2 = -1.
$$

However, this is equivalent to 

$$
    \int_{0}^{1} dp f(p) \mathcal{C}_{2,-p^2}^{\text{improved}}\ge 0.
$$
for $ J = 0, 2, \cdots , J_{\text{max}} $.
